University Transfer

Lakeland University

Lakeland University offers three bachelor's degree options for Blackhawk students who have earned their assocate degree in Accounting, Early Childhood Education, Foundations of Teacher Education, Web Software Developer and IT-Technical Support Specialist. Students can transfer into these corresponding bachelor's degree programs with a 20% discount:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Accounting
  • Bachelor's Degree in Elementary and Middle Schooln Education
  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science

Internships and Career Connections

Blackhawk Tech is partnered with local businesses which allows for many students to have an opportunity to complete paid internships.
