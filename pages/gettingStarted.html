How to enroll in BTC:

Go to Blackhawk.edu. In the top right corner, click on "Apply".

On the "Welcome to Blackhawk" screen, click the "Create Account" link.

A form with multiple text boxes will appear. Fill in the required information indicated by asterisks (*). Continue through the subsequent screens, filling in all relevant fields (optional fields are recommended but not mandatory).

How to register for classes:

After being accepted and meeting with your advisor (Devin Timblin), go back to Blackhawk.edu. This time, click “MyBTC.” You may be prompted to sign in. Sign in using your @students.blackhawk.edu email address.

Once signed in, click the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top left corner.

Click “Discover.”

In the “Find cards” search bar, type “Registration.”

Find the Registration card and scroll about halfway down the page. Then, click the “Register Now” link.

You may be prompted to complete a short survey. Complete the survey, then click “Continue.” Once you reach the Ellucian main page, click the “Register for Classes” link.

Select the correct term from the dropdown menu, then click “Continue.”

You can begin typing the class name or course number. Some classes have different sessions/times, so make sure you select the correct session (time/MyEdChoice method) if using this search method. Entering the CRN (Course Reference Number) is preferred. Click the search button, select the correct option, and repeat this process for all your classes.

When finished entering all your classes, click the grey “Submit” button in the bottom right corner.

For a simpler class search, click the “Enter CRNs” button on the navigation bar. Then, enter the CRN for your class (this number will follow the format xxx-xxx). Click “Add to Summary” and repeat the process for all your classes.

Once finished, click the grey “Submit” button in the bottom right corner.
